Our Story

Born From the
Spirit of the Island

Tales of the Islands began as a dream — a place where the warmth of Filipino hospitality meets the unhurried rhythm of El Nido. Every dish is a small story. Every cup of coffee, a quiet ritual. Every morning here, a reminder that some things are best savored slowly.

"We didn't just open a café. We created a sanctuary — a place to belong between island adventures."

Rooted in the natural beauty of Palawan, we source fresh, local ingredients and craft each plate with intention. From our signature island breakfasts to our hand-poured local coffee, everything here is made with love and the unhurried care of island life.

Our Philosophy

Food Grown
With Intention

We believe good food starts before the kitchen. We partner with local farmers and communities across Palawan — bringing you the freshest tropical produce, caught-that-morning seafood, and ingredients with stories worth telling.

"Simple ingredients. Honest hands. The flavors of an island morning."

At Tales of the Islands, sustainability isn't a policy — it's a feeling. The way we cook reflects our respect for this land, the ocean, and the people who tend it.
